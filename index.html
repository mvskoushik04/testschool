Welcome to Test School

Top ranking school

Recognised as Hyderabad & Telangana's 3rd Best International Day School (2023–25) by Education World and ranked 16th nationally, Test School provides a transformative learning environment for students.

Top university placements

Test School students consistently earn placements at prestigious universities worldwide – including Stanford University, Imperial College London and London School of Economics.

Life skills

Students develop communication, digital fluency and self-leadership through a Cambridge-backed life skills framework, with personalised lessons, Quality Circle Time and Bubble Talks.

Here's what sets Test School apart

  • Unwavering Dedication to Quality Education: With a proven track record of academic success, we offer a rigorous curriculum that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.
  • Nurturing Values and Character: We go beyond academics, instilling strong moral values and social consciousness in our students, preparing them to be responsible and empathetic members of society.
  • Embracing Technology: Recognizing the crucial role of technology in the modern world, we integrate cutting-edge tools and resources into our teaching, ensuring our students are equipped for the future.
  • Supportive and Dedicated Community: Our vibrant school community thrives on the collaboration of passionate educators, caring staff, and supportive families, creating a nurturing environment for every child to blossom.
